Why LiveShot

What's at Stake

AI-generated briefings on every match: ranking points on the line, defending points, H2H on this surface, recent form, the storylines that matter.

Behind this Match

A second AI card unpacks the rivalry — how these two have met before, the surface dynamics, the moment from their last meeting you'd want to know.

Spoiler Mode

Planning to watch a replay? Toggle Spoiler Mode globally or per-player. Scores hide in the app and notifications get rewritten to drop the score.

Follow your players — when their match starts, your lock screen and Dynamic Island stream the score automatically. No app open required.
